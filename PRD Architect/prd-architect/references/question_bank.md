# Question Bank - PRD Architect

Use small batches (5-8 questions). Prefer questions that change requirements or reveal missing scope.

## Stage 1

1. Who is the target user and how large is that segment?
2. What problem does this feature solve, and how is it observed today?
3. What is the business goal (growth, retention, revenue, cost)?
4. What is the primary success signal?
5. What constraints or dependencies already exist?

## Stage 2

1. What user role is most impacted?
2. What action should the user take?
3. What concrete benefit should they receive?
4. What is the main job to be done behind the story?

## Stage 3

1. What must the system do step by step?
2. What is the minimum viable behavior?
3. What states does the system create or change?
4. What is the acceptance criteria for each core requirement?

## Stage 4

1. What happens on invalid input or missing permissions?
2. What happens when the network or service fails?
3. What happens on duplicates, retries, or timeouts?
4. What are the hard limits (size, rate, quota)?

## Stage 5

1. What latency, throughput, or availability targets are required?
2. What security or privacy requirements apply?
3. What data retention or audit needs exist?

## Stage 6

1. What metrics prove user success?
2. What metrics prove business success?
3. What is the target threshold and timeframe?
4. What data source will be used?
