# Analysis Checklist

Use this to ensure completeness and consistency.

## Completeness

- Goal and primary metrics are defined.
- Baseline period is defined.
- Trends are identified with magnitude and direction.
- Behavioral analysis covers funnel and activation.
- Hypotheses are testable and linked to signals.
- Experiments include success criteria.
- Data gaps are explicitly listed.

## Consistency

- Conclusions map to evidence.
- Hypotheses align with observed trends.
- Experiments target the highest-uncertainty hypotheses.
