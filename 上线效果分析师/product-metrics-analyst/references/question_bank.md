# Question Bank - Product Metrics Analyst

Use small batches (5-8 questions). Prefer questions that change conclusions or identify missing data.

## Stage 1

1. What is the primary goal of this feature or release?
2. Which metrics define success (adoption, retention, revenue, efficiency)?
3. Who is the target user segment?
4. What is the baseline period for comparison?

## Stage 2

1. What are the current values for core metrics?
2. What cohorts or segments matter most (new vs returning, region, plan)?
3. Which versions or channels should be compared?
4. Are there known data quality issues?

## Stage 3

1. Which metrics show significant change vs baseline?
2. Are any changes localized to a segment, version, or channel?
3. What is the magnitude and duration of the change?

## Stage 4

1. What does the primary funnel look like?
2. Where is the largest drop-off?
3. Are users reaching the core value moment?
4. Which features are used most/least after activation?

## Stage 5

1. What is the simplest explanation for the top trend?
2. What supporting signals exist?
3. What would disconfirm the hypothesis?

## Stage 6

1. What experiment could reduce the biggest uncertainty?
2. What is the success criterion and timeframe?
3. What metric should move if the hypothesis is correct?
